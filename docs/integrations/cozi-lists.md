# Cozi Lists Integration

Display shopping and to-do lists from your Cozi account.

**Type:** Integration (Task List)

## Overview
Sync your Cozi lists to BrandCast to keep family or team tasks visible.

## Connection
1.  Go to **Integrations** > **Cozi Lists**.
2.  Enter your Cozi credentials (email/password).

## Configuration (Content Source)
1.  **Create Source:** Select **Create New Source**.
2.  **Select List:** Choose which Cozi list to display (e.g., "Grocery", "To Do").
3.  **Options:**
    *   **Show Checked Items:** Toggle visibility of completed items.
    *   **Styling:** Choose list styles (checkboxes, bullet points).

## Usage
1.  **Add to Layout:** In Layout Editor, add a **Task List** area and select your Cozi source.
